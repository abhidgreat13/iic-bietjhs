Introduction

Siege Of Tyre is a fun and exciting competitive event organized by Innovation and Incubation cell under INNOVANZA 3.0 for the young innovators. It’ll provide them with a platform to transform their ideas into a working model of a “TREBUCHET”.


Flow of Event


Introduction:
All the participants will be given a brief introduction about “what”, “how” and “when” of the event along with its rules and regulations. If required they will also be mentored about Trebuchet working and its designing and making skills.

Task Distribution:
The participants will be briefed about the TREBUCHET model they have to make along with its dimensional specifications.

Team Building:
The participants will be allowed to interact and make teams, each team can have 4-5 members.

Working Time:
The teams are supposed to work on their model for 7 days. In this time the mentors will come to ask about their progress and will resolve their issues if any.

Presentation and Working:
After completing their models, teams will present them and will demonstrate its working according to different judging parameters.

Judging:
The models made by each team will be judged on the basis of different criterions.

Prize Distribution:
The jury will decide the winner in accordance to set parameters and they will be awarded.
